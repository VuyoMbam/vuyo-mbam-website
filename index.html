About Me

Currently a researcher at South Africa’s Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) Energy Centre, my primary focus is supporting South Africa’s just energy transition, ensuring the shift from fossil fuels to cleaner energy sources is sustainable and equitable.

My work involves:

  • Modeling the costs and benefits of transitioning to renewable energy,
  • Value chain analysis for technologies such as renewable energy and energy storage technologies,
  • Energy system modelling for systems with high shares of renewables, and
  • research on the emerging hydrogen economy and circularity within the energy sector.

I hold a Master of Science in Renewable Energy from Cranfield University and a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechatronics from Nelson Mandela University.
